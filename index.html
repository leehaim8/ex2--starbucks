About us

Starbucks was founded in 1971 in Seattle's Pike Place Market by three friends who wanted to create a "third place" between home and work.

The company's name was inspired by the character of Starbuck, a mate on the Pequod in the novel Moby Dick.

The original Starbucks siren logo was much more graphic, but it was changed in 1987 to the more familiar version that is seen today.
